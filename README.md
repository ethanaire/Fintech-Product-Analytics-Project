# Fintech Product Analytics Project

## I. Business Context Mastery 

### 1. Clarify the Business Problem

- **Business Objective**:
  - Identify and analyze the root causes behind the **decline in success rate** on the e-wallet platform.

  - Propose actionable solutions to restore and improve the success rate, user experience, and business performance.

- **Key Metrics / KPIs**:
  - Success Rate = Number of successful transactions / Total transactions
  
- **Stakeholders**:
  - Product Owners

  - Engineering & Backend Teams
  - Data & BI Analysts
  - Customer Support
  - Marketing & Retention Teams
 
### 2. Decode the Industry

Industry-specific Challenges:
  - Rapidly growing transaction volume puts stress on backend systems and third-party payment gateways.

  - Stricter security and regulatory requirements (OTP, 3DS, multi-factor authentication) add friction to user flows.
  - Diverse user base (age, device, digital literacy) impacts product usability and support needs.
  - Multiple billers/providers with varying integration and backend requirements.

### 3. Identify Key Stakeholders
  - Product/UX Teams: Need insights to optimize the payment journey and reduce drop-off.
  
  - Engineering: Prioritize resolving authentication, app performance, and gateway issues.
  - BI/Data Teams: Analyze data, monitor KPIs, and deliver early warnings.
  - Support: Reduce support tickets by minimizing transaction failures.
  - Management: Focus on restoring key business metrics and competitive position.
